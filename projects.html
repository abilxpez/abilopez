About Exhibition

[Expo Title] is a collection of art created by members of the Stanford community to explore their immigrant identity. The collection includes poems, essays, paintings, performances, and even computer science projects that share community narratives from many immigrant backgrounds. This exhibition creates space for important discourse and encourages viewers to reimagine identity and immigration. But more importantly, [Expo Title] empowers contributors to explore their identity and reclaim their narrative. I hope to present the art exposition near the end of Spring Quarter at White Plaza, Stanford.
